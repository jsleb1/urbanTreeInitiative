Our Story

It all started with a shovel and a dream. Several years later we're making a great presence as we do our best to revitalize the urban areas of Western New York.


We recognize that along with urban development comes a lot of unused, unkept land that is either left as bare grassland or empty concrete lots.

Urban Tree Initiative is dedicated to revitalizing these areas by planting trees in an effort to beautify, as well as appropriately repurpose, these pieces of land.
